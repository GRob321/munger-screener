# Announcing the Munger 200-Week Screener

Charlie Munger once said: *"If all you ever did was buy high-quality stocks on the 200-week moving average, you would beat the S&P 500 by a large margin over time."* 

I kept hearing this idea but never tested it. So I decided to run the numbers.

## The Results

I backtested this strategy from 2000 to 2024 against the S&P 500. The results speak for themselves: **buying quality companies at their 200-week moving average significantly outperformed the market**. Not by luck. By discipline.

The 200-week MA works because:

- It filters out hype and noise
- It finds the best companies when they're reasonably priced
- It removes emotion from the decision
- It's simple enough to actually stick with

## Introducing the Munger 200-Week Screener

I built a tool to automate this. Enter any ticker, and it tells you:

- Is this a quality company? (fundamental analysis)
- Where does it stand relative to its 200-week moving average?
- Should you buy, wait, or avoid?

The goal is simple: take Munger's principle and make it accessible. No complex models. No market timing. Just quality + price discipline.
